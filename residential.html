Homes of Distinction

Hickman Homes is a builder who blends the tradition of fine home building with the latest in new home technology. The result is light, bright homes that are a pleasure to live in, easy to maintain, and offered at a reasonable cost. These are homes that will not go out of style, homes that will be appreciated more and more each passing year!

In addition to building these distinctively better homes on sites throughout Polk County, Hickman Homes is creating neighborhoods. Our upcoming work in Lakeland includes "Heritage Park, a traditional neighborhood with a 7-acre community park. Also look for us at "Grandefield at Poley Creek", Morgan Creek Preserve, Highlands-in-the-Woods, Hallam Preserve, Water's Ridge and other fine communities that offer those extra special amenities. These neighborhoods and others enable us to showcase the charm and uniqueness of the homes we build. These are neighborhoods where you can express your own personality in the home that you live in and enjoy a close knit community for you and your family.
