Hickman Homes goes Green with the "Go Green" Showcase Home in Lakeland, FL

Hickman Homes is a pioneer in Green construction in Lakeland and Polk County, FL. Our award-winning home in Morgan Creek Preserve takes the best of all aspects of green building and incorporates it into one home to save the homeowner a considerable amount of money in energy costs while also reducing harm to the environment.

Landscaping Mother Nature would love

Through a collaborative effort with the Polk County Extension Service and the South West Florida Management District (SWFMD) the "Go Green" Showcase Home was one of the first homes in central Florida to receive the "Water Star Gold Certification" and the Florida Friendly Yard certification. Used for training purposes, the landscape features low flow irrigation, drought tolerant Zoysia grass, Florida native plants, a cistern for storm water collection, permeable pavers with in-ground drainage system and a host of other water conserving features, inside and outside the home.
